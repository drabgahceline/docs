# Gear

Gear adds power to your soldiers. Find it through training, equip it, and dominate battles.

## How Gear Works

- Each soldier can equip **up to 3 gear items**
- Each gear piece adds a **flat power bonus**
- Power bonus applies to all combat (attack and defense)

<Note>
Gear simply makes your soldier stronger. There's no separate attack vs defense stats â€” just raw power.
</Note>

## Getting Gear

### Training Drops

Gear drops from **active training** based on your accuracy grade:

| Grade | Drop Chance |
|-------|-------------|
| Perfect (85%+) | 30% |
| Great (70%+) | 10% |
| Good (55%+) | 7% |
| OK | 5% |

<Tip>
Passive training also has a chance to drop gear, but at a lower rate.
</Tip>

### Commander Level Rewards

Specific named gear unlocks at certain commander levels. Once unlocked, these items enter your drop pool with fixed power values (often better than random drops).

### Bundles

Some soldier bundles include guaranteed gear pieces.

---

## Equipping Gear

1. Go to **Soldiers** page
2. Select a soldier
3. Click the **Gear** tab
4. Click an item to equip or unequip

<Warning>
Each gear item can only be equipped to **one soldier** at a time. Unequip first to move it.
</Warning>
