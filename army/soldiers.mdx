# Your Soldiers

Every soldier in Cronos Army is a unique NFT with its own stats, personality, and history.

## Soldier Attributes

Each soldier has:

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Name** | Unique generated name |
| **Rarity** | Common â†’ Legendary (affects base stats) |
| **Power** | Combat strength in battles |
| **Health** | How much damage they can take |
| **Personality** | Affects special ability and dialogue |
| **Energy** | Current battle readiness |

## Viewing Your Army

In the Command Center, you can:
- See all your soldiers
- Check their stats and energy
- View their battle history
- Read their memories and rivalries

## Soldier Actions

**Deploy to Battle**
Select soldiers for territory attacks or defenses.

**Rest & Recover**
Low energy soldiers can recover passively or use credits for instant restore.

**View History**
See who they've fought, who they've killed, and their developing rivalries.
