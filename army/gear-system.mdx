## Getting Gear

### Training Drops

Gear can drop after completing training:

| Training Type | Drop Chance |
|---------------|-------------|
| Active Training | 10% base (modified by grade) |
| Passive Training | 10% base |

Facility bonuses (Forge) increase drop chances.

### Mission Rewards

Missions can reward gear on success:

| Mission Difficulty | Legendary Chance |
|--------------------|------------------|
| Easy | 0% |
| Medium | 0% |
| Hard | 5% |
| Legendary | 20% |

### Events & Achievements

Special gear may be awarded during:
- Community events
- Tournament prizes
- Achievement milestones

## Equipping Gear

1. Go to **Soldiers** page
2. Select a soldier
3. Click **Gear** tab
4. Click an item to equip/unequip
5. Power updates immediately

<Warning>
Each gear item can only be equipped to ONE soldier at a time. Unequip first to move it.
</Warning>
