# Credits

Credits are the in-game currency for convenience and speed.

## What Credits Do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Instant Energy" icon="bolt">
    Restore soldier energy immediately instead of waiting.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Speed Actions" icon="forward">
    Accelerate recovery and other time-gated actions.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting Credits

| Source | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| **Welcome Bonus** | 10 free credits when claiming soldiers |
| **Purchase** | Buy directly with CRO in-game |

## Using Credits Wisely

Credits are for **convenience**, not requirement. Everything in the game can be done without spending credits — it just takes longer.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Good Times to Use Credits">
    - Critical territory defense incoming
    - Tournament or event participation
    - You want to play more, faster
    - Limited time to play today
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Save Credits When">
    - Normal daily gameplay — let energy regen naturally
    - No urgent battles pending
    - You're still learning the game
    - You have backup soldiers ready
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
New players: use your 10 free credits to learn the game, not to rush through it. You'll make better decisions about spending once you understand the mechanics.
</Tip>
