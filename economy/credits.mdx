# Credits

Credits are the in-game currency for convenience and speed. They're **optional** — everything in the game can be done without them.

## What Credits Do

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Extra Training" icon="dumbbell">
    Train soldiers beyond the daily free session.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Instant Energy" icon="bolt">
    Restore soldier energy immediately.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Speed Upgrades" icon="forward">
    Skip facility upgrade wait times.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Skip Timers" icon="clock">
    Complete passive training instantly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting Credits

| Source | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| **Welcome Bonus** | 10 free credits when claiming soldiers |
| **Purchase** | Buy with CRO in-game ([see bundles](/economy/credit-shop)) |
| **Events** | Occasional tournament/event rewards |

## Quick Tips

<Tip>
**New players:** Use your 10 free credits to learn the game. Don't rush — you'll make better spending decisions once you understand the mechanics.
</Tip>

<Note>
Credits aren't required for anything. Patient players can achieve everything without spending.
</Note>
