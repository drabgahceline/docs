## What Credits Buy

### Energy Recovery

Restore soldier energy instantly instead of waiting.

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Energy recovery | 0.05 credits per 1% |
| Full recharge (0→100%) | ~5 credits |

**Example:** Restore 50% energy = 2.5 credits

### Training Speed-Up

Skip the 24-hour wait on passive training.

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Instant complete (any training) | 1 credit flat |

<Note>
Instant complete is a flat 1 credit regardless of time remaining. Use it when you need soldiers NOW.
</Note>

### Facility Upgrades

Speed up territory facility construction.

| Action | Cost |
|--------|------|
| Instant facility upgrade | Varies by level |

## Spending Strategy

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="When to spend credits">
    - Time-sensitive defenses (territory under attack)
    - Tournament participation
    - You want to play more NOW
    - Limited playtime today
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When to save credits">
    - Normal daily gameplay
    - No urgent battles
    - You're still learning
    - You have backup soldiers
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Best value actions">
    1. **Instant training complete** (1 credit) — best ROI if you play actively
    2. **Emergency defense recharge** — save a valuable territory
    3. **Batch energy restore** — before a big session
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Buy

1. Go to **Command Center**
2. Click **Buy Credits**
3. Select your bundle
4. Confirm the CRO transaction
5. Credits added instantly

Credits are tied to your wallet address and persist across sessions.
