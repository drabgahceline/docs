# Multi-Chain Play

Cronos Army is available on both **Cronos** and **BNB Chain**. Choose the chain that works best for you.

## Key Differences

| Feature | Cronos | BNB Chain |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| Gas token | CRO | BNB |
| Gas fees | ~0.01 CRO | ~0.001 BNB |
| CA liquidity | Higher | Growing |
| NFT ecosystem | Cronos native | BNB ecosystem |
| Player base | Original community | New expansion |

<Note>
Game mechanics, rewards, and progression are **identical** on both chains. Only the underlying tokens differ.
</Note>

## Getting Started on Each Chain

### Cronos Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Cronos Network">
    Add Cronos to your wallet:
    - **RPC URL:** https://evm.cronos.org
    - **Chain ID:** 25
    - **Symbol:** CRO
    - **Explorer:** https://cronoscan.com
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get CRO">
    Purchase CRO on exchanges (Crypto.com, KuCoin) and transfer to your wallet
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get CA Tokens">
    Swap CRO for CA on DEXs like VVS Finance or Ferro Protocol
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect & Play">
    Visit croarmy.site, connect wallet, start playing
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choosing Your Chain

### Choose Cronos If:

- You're already in the Cronos ecosystem
- You want deeper CA liquidity
- You prefer the original player community
- You hold CRO or use Crypto.com

### Choose BNB Chain If:

- You're already in the BNB ecosystem
- You have BNB ready to go
- You want lower gas fees
- You prefer the larger DEX ecosystem

## Cross-Chain Considerations

### Soldiers Are Chain-Specific

Your soldiers are NFTs on a specific chain:
- Cronos soldiers exist on Cronos
- BNB soldiers exist on BNB Chain
- They cannot be transferred between chains

### Progress Is Chain-Specific

Your gameplay progress (territories, facilities, DP) is tied to your wallet on each chain. Playing on both chains means managing two separate accounts.

### CA Token Is Bridgeable

The CA token can be bridged between chains using supported bridges. This allows you to move CA liquidity between chains if needed.

<Note>
If you want to switch chains mid-game, you'll need to start fresh on the new chain. Consider this before choosing.
</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I play on both chains with the same wallet?">
    Yes. Your wallet address works on both chains. But your soldiers, territories, and progress are separate on each.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are rewards the same on both chains?">
    Yes. WPP leaderboards, conquest rewards, and all game mechanics are identical.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which chain has more players?">
    Cronos has the original and larger player base. BNB Chain is growing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I bridge my soldier NFTs?">
    No. Soldier NFTs are chain-specific and cannot be bridged.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if a chain has issues?">
    Each chain operates independently. Issues on one chain don't affect the other.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
