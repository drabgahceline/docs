<Info>
Need help? The community and team are active on Discord. Post your question in the general chat or check the [FAQ](/getting-started/faq) first.

**For bug reports**: Use the `#bug-report` channel on Discord with:
- What happened
- What you expected
- Your wallet address (if relevant)
- Screenshots if possible
