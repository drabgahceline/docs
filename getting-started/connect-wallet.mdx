# Connect Your Wallet

Ready to receive your soldiers? You'll need a Web3 wallet on the Cronos network.

## Recommended Wallets

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="MetaMask" icon="mask" href="https://metamask.io">
    The most popular Web3 wallet. Works on desktop and mobile.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Crypto.com DeFi Wallet" icon="wallet" href="https://crypto.com/defi-wallet">
    Native Cronos support. Great for beginners.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Setting Up MetaMask

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install MetaMask">
    Download from [metamask.io](https://metamask.io) for your browser or phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Wallet">
    Follow the setup wizard. **Save your seed phrase somewhere safe!**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Cronos Network">
    Click the network dropdown and select "Add Network". Use these settings:
    
    | Setting | Value |
    |---------|-------|
    | Network Name | Cronos |
    | RPC URL | https://evm.cronos.org |
    | Chain ID | 25 |
    | Symbol | CRO |
    | Explorer | https://cronoscan.com |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Some CRO">
    You'll need a small amount of CRO for gas fees. Buy on [Crypto.com](https://crypto.com) or any major exchange.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Claim Your Soldiers

Once your wallet is set up:

1. Click the claim link in your email (or visit croarmy.site)
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Approve the connection in MetaMask
4. Your soldiers transfer instantly!

<Check>
  **You also receive 10 bonus credits** when you complete your claim.
</Check>
