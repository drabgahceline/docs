# Connect Your Wallet

To receive your soldiers and start earning, you'll need a Web3 wallet on the Cronos network.

<Note>
Already have a Cronos wallet? Skip to [Claim Your Soldiers](#claim-your-soldiers) below.
</Note>

## Setting Up MetaMask

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install MetaMask">
    Download from [metamask.io](https://metamask.io) for your browser or mobile device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Wallet">
    Follow the setup wizard. **Write down your seed phrase and store it safely!**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Cronos Network">
    MetaMask defaults to Ethereum. Add Cronos with these settings:
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Network Name** | Cronos |
| **RPC URL** | `https://evm.cronos.org` |
| **Chain ID** | `25` |
| **Symbol** | `CRO` |
| **Explorer** | `https://cronoscan.com` |

<Tip>
Most wallets auto-detect Cronos when you connect to croarmy.site — you may not need to add it manually.
</Tip>

## Get Some CRO

You'll need a small amount of CRO for transaction fees (gas). Options:

- **Crypto.com App** — Buy CRO and withdraw to your wallet
- **Exchanges** — Buy on any exchange that lists CRO
- **Bridge** — Bridge from other chains via [Crypto.com DeFi Wallet](https://crypto.com/defi-wallet)

<Note>
Gas fees on Cronos are very low — a few cents per transaction. You don't need much CRO to play.
</Note>

## Claim Your Soldiers

Once your wallet is ready:

1. Click the claim link in your email (or visit [croarmy.site](https://croarmy.site))
2. Click **Connect Wallet**
3. Approve the connection in your wallet
4. Your soldiers transfer instantly!

You'll also receive **10 bonus credits** when you complete your claim.
