# Your First Battle

You've got soldiers. Time to put them to work.

## Enter the Battlefield

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Battlefield">
    Click **Battlefield** in the navigation to see the territory map.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a Target">
    Look for territories under siege (flashing) or pick an enemy territory to attack.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Your Soldiers">
    Select which soldiers to send. Check their energy levels first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fight!">
    Enter battle and take command of your forces.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Battle Basics

Cronos Army uses **turn-based card combat**. You control every decision.

### Each Turn You Choose:

1. **Which soldier** attacks
2. **What action** they take
3. **Who they target**

### Combat Actions

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Attack" icon="sword">
    Maximum damage. No protection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Defensive" icon="shield">
    Deal less damage, but take less too.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Special" icon="star">
    Unique ability based on personality.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Personality Powers

Your soldier's personality determines their special move:

| Personality | Special | Effect |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| **Aggressive** | Piercing Strike | Ignores enemy defense |
| **Intelligent** | Analyze | +40% damage |
| **Reckless** | All-In | 2x damage (risky) |
| **Defensive** | Shield Wall | -60% damage taken |

<Tip>
Focus fire on one enemy at a time. A dead enemy deals no damage.
</Tip>

## Victory & Rewards

**Win the battle:**
- Capture or defend the territory
- Earn **25 WPP** (5 base + 20 victory bonus)
- Territory generates CRO while you hold it

**Lose the battle:**
- Still earn **5 WPP** for participating
- Learn from defeat and try again

<Note>
Even losing battles earn you WPP. Stay active to climb the weekly leaderboard.
</Note>

## What's Next?

After your first battle, you'll understand the basics. From here:

- **Hold territories** to earn passive CRO income
- **Battle regularly** to accumulate WPP for weekly rewards
- **Expand your army** with stronger soldiers
