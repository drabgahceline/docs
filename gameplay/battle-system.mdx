## Enemy AI Behavior

When defending, enemy soldiers respond based on their personalities:

- **Aggressive** defenders hunt your weakest soldier
- **Stubborn** defenders attack whoever hit them last
- **Intelligent** defenders focus your biggest threat
- **Defensive** defenders protect their wounded allies

Understanding these patterns helps you predict enemy moves.

## Card Battles (Siege Combat)

When attacking or defending territories, combat uses the **card battle** system â€” a tactical showdown between soldier cards.

### Card Battle Overview

- **Health** â€” How much damage they can take

- **Personality** â€” Determines their special ability
- **Special Move** â€” One-time powerful ability (once per battle)

### Siege Card Combat

1. **Attacker deploys** up to 3 soldier cards
2. **Defender's cards** are already assigned to the territory

### Progressive Siege

Territory sieges are **progressive** â€” damage carries over:

1. **Each battle chips away** at defender/attacker health
2. **Multiple attacks** may be needed to capture
3. **Defenders can reinforce** between attacks
4. **Territory falls** when all defenders are eliminated

This creates strategic depth â€” do you go all-in or wear them down over time?
