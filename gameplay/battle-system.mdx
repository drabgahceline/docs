# Battle System

Cronos Army uses a **3v3 turn-based card battle** system. You pick 3 soldiers, the enemy has 3 soldiers, and you fight until one team is eliminated.

## Enemy AI Targeting

When defending, enemy soldiers pick targets based on personality:

| Personality | Targeting Logic |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Aggressive** | Attacks your lowest HP soldier |
| **Intelligent** | Attacks your highest power soldier |
| **Stubborn** | Counter-attacks whoever hit them |
| **Zombie** | Targets lowest HP (going for kills) |
| **Others** | Random or first available |

Understanding AI patterns helps you predict enemy moves and protect vulnerable soldiers.
