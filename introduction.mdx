# Welcome, Commander

Cronos Army is a Web3 strategy game where you command an army of AI-powered soldiers to conquer territories and earn rewards.

**This isn't your typical blockchain game.** Your soldiers think. They speak. They remember.

## What Makes Cronos Army Different

  <Card title="Living Soldiers" icon="brain">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Real Strategy" icon="chess">
    Turn-based card combat where your decisions matter. Choose attacks, pick targets, and outmaneuver your opponents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Earn While Playing" icon="coins">
